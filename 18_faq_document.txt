FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
============================
Product: CloudSync Pro v4.2

Q: How do I install CloudSync Pro?
A: Download the installer from our website, run the .exe or .dmg file,
   and follow the on-screen instructions. Requires 500MB free space.

Q: Is my data encrypted?
A: Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).

Q: How many devices can I sync?
A: Free plan: 2 devices. Pro plan: unlimited devices.

Q: What happens if I exceed my storage limit?
A: Uploads will pause. You'll receive an email notification 3 days before
   hitting the limit. Upgrade your plan to continue syncing.

Q: Can I share files with non-users?
A: Yes. Generate a shareable link from any file. Links expire in 7 days
   by default (configurable in settings).

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Account > Billing > Cancel Subscription. You retain access
   until the end of your billing period.
