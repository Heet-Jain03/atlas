STUDY NOTES: World War II - Key Events
========================================
Course: Modern History 201

CAUSES OF WWII:
- Treaty of Versailles (1919) — harsh penalties on Germany
- Rise of fascism in Europe (Hitler, Mussolini)
- Global Great Depression (economic instability)
- Failure of appeasement policy

TIMELINE OF KEY EVENTS:
1939  Germany invades Poland → UK/France declare war
1940  Fall of France; Battle of Britain begins
1941  Germany invades USSR; Japan attacks Pearl Harbor → US enters war
1942  Battle of Midway; Battle of Stalingrad
1944  D-Day — Allied invasion of Normandy (June 6)
1945  Germany surrenders (May 8 — V-E Day)
      Atomic bombs dropped on Hiroshima & Nagasaki
      Japan surrenders (Sept 2 — V-J Day)

DEATH TOLL: Approx. 70–85 million (military + civilian)

KEY FIGURES: Churchill, Roosevelt, Stalin, Hitler, Eisenhower, MacArthur
