WEEKLY WORKOUT PLAN
====================
Goal: Build strength and improve cardio
Level: Intermediate

MONDAY - Upper Body Strength
  Push-ups        3 sets x 15 reps
  Dumbbell rows   3 sets x 12 reps
  Shoulder press  3 sets x 10 reps
  Bicep curls     3 sets x 12 reps

TUESDAY - Cardio
  30-minute run (moderate pace)
  10-minute cool-down walk

WEDNESDAY - Lower Body
  Squats          4 sets x 15 reps
  Lunges          3 sets x 12 reps each leg
  Deadlifts       3 sets x 10 reps
  Calf raises     3 sets x 20 reps

THURSDAY - Rest or light yoga

FRIDAY - Full Body HIIT (30 minutes)
SATURDAY - Outdoor activity (hike, swim, bike)
SUNDAY - Complete rest

NOTES: Stay hydrated. Sleep 7–8 hours. Track progress weekly.
