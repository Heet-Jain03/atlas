CLASSIC PANCAKES RECIPE
========================
Serves: 4 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 20 min

INGREDIENTS:
- 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
- 3.5 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp white sugar
- 1.25 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp melted butter

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
2. Make a well in the center and pour in wet ingredients.
3. Mix until smooth.
4. Heat a pan over medium-high heat and lightly oil it.
5. Pour batter onto pan (about 1/4 cup per pancake).
6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
7. Cook until golden brown on both sides.

Serve with maple syrup and fresh berries. Enjoy!
