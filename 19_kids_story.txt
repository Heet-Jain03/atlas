BEDTIME STORY: The Brave Little Cloud
=======================================
Once upon a time, high above the mountains, there lived a small cloud
named Cirrus. While the big gray clouds boomed with thunder and flashed
with lightning, little Cirrus could only make the tiniest sprinkle.

"You're too small to be a real rain cloud," the big clouds rumbled.

Cirrus felt sad. But one hot summer day, a little girl named Maya sat
in her garden, her flowers drooping in the heat.

"I wish for just a little rain," Maya whispered.

Cirrus floated over and squeezed with all his might. A gentle shower
fell — just enough to make the flowers stand tall and Maya smile.

"Thank you, little cloud!" Maya called up.

From that day on, Cirrus knew that small things could still do big good.

THE END. Sweet dreams.
