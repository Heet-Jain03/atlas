30 AMAZING FUN FACTS
=====================

1.  Honey never spoils — edible honey was found in 3,000-year-old tombs.
2.  A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance."
3.  Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood.
4.  The Eiffel Tower grows ~15cm taller in summer (metal expands in heat).
5.  Bananas are berries; strawberries are not (botanically speaking).
6.  Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the pyramids.
7.  A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus.
8.  The human body contains enough carbon to make ~9,000 pencils.
9.  Sharks are older than trees (sharks: 450M years; trees: 350M years).
10. Wombat poop is cube-shaped.
11. Nintendo was founded in 1889 — as a playing card company.
12. The average person walks about 100,000 miles in their lifetime.
13. Crows can recognize and remember human faces.
14. There are more possible chess games than atoms in the universe.
15. A bolt of lightning is 5x hotter than the surface of the sun.
16. Sea otters hold hands while sleeping so they don't drift apart.
17. The word "robot" comes from a Czech word meaning "forced labor."
18. Penguins propose with pebbles.
19. The moon is drifting away from Earth at ~3.8 cm per year.
20. It rains diamonds on Neptune and Uranus.
21. A snail can sleep for 3 years.
22. Butterflies taste with their feet.
23. Glass is technically a very slow-moving liquid.
24. The shortest war in history lasted 38–45 minutes (Anglo-Zanzibar, 1896).
25. Your nose can detect over 1 trillion different smells.
26. Cats have fewer toes on their back paws than front paws.
27. Pineapple plants take 18–24 months to produce just one fruit.
28. There is a species of jellyfish considered biologically immortal.
29. The dot over the letters 'i' and 'j' is called a "tittle."
30. Oxford University is older than the Aztec Empire.
