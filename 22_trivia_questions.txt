TRIVIA NIGHT - Question Set #7
================================
Category: Mixed Bag

1. What is the capital of Australia?
   Answer: Canberra

2. Which planet has the most moons in our solar system?
   Answer: Saturn (146 confirmed moons as of 2024)

3. Who painted the Sistine Chapel ceiling?
   Answer: Michelangelo

4. In what year did the Berlin Wall fall?
   Answer: 1989

5. What is the chemical symbol for gold?
   Answer: Au

6. How many bones are in the adult human body?
   Answer: 206

7. What language has the most native speakers in the world?
   Answer: Mandarin Chinese

8. Which country invented pizza?
   Answer: Italy (Naples)

9. What is the longest river in the world?
   Answer: The Nile (approx. 6,650 km)

10. Who wrote "Romeo and Juliet"?
    Answer: William Shakespeare
