HOUSEPLANT CARE GUIDE
======================

1. POTHOS (Easy - Beginner Friendly)
   Water:  Every 1–2 weeks; let soil dry out
   Light:  Low to bright indirect light
   Tip:    Nearly indestructible. Great starter plant!

2. MONSTERA DELICIOSA
   Water:  Every 1–2 weeks in summer, less in winter
   Light:  Bright indirect light
   Tip:    Wipe leaves monthly to remove dust

3. SNAKE PLANT (Sansevieria)
   Water:  Every 2–6 weeks (very drought tolerant)
   Light:  Any light condition
   Tip:    Overwatering is the #1 killer — when in doubt, wait

4. PEACE LILY
   Water:  Weekly; likes moist but not soggy soil
   Light:  Low to medium indirect light
   Tip:    Drooping leaves = needs water. It'll tell you!

5. FIDDLE LEAF FIG
   Water:  Weekly in growing season
   Light:  Bright indirect light, consistent spot
   Tip:    Hates being moved. Find a spot and leave it there.
