BIRTHDAY SPEECH FOR MOM'S 60TH
================================
Good evening everyone!

For those who don't know me, I'm Jamie, the proudest child in this room.
Sixty years ago, the world got a whole lot better — and I don't say that
lightly.

Mom, you have been my rock, my cheerleader, my chef, my therapist (sorry
for the teenage years), and my best friend. Every milestone in my life
has your fingerprints on it.

You've taught me that kindness is never weakness, that homemade food
cures most problems, and that it's okay to cry at commercials.

So please, everyone, raise your glasses.

To a woman who is somehow more vibrant, more adventurous, and more
stylish at sixty than most people are at thirty.

To MOM — Happy Birthday. We love you more than words can say!

*cheers*
