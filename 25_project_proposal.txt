PROJECT PROPOSAL
=================
Project Title: Community Garden Revitalization
Submitted by:  Green Spaces Initiative
Date:          April 21, 2026

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:
We propose a 6-month project to revitalize the vacant lot on Elm Street
into a functional community garden serving the local neighborhood.

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
The 0.4-acre lot has been unused for 4 years, attracting litter and
reducing neighborhood appeal. Meanwhile, 68% of residents in surveys
expressed interest in access to fresh produce and green space.

PROPOSED SOLUTION:
- Clear and landscape the lot (Month 1)
- Install raised garden beds — 20 plots (Month 2)
- Community planting day & orientation (Month 3)
- Ongoing programming: workshops, kids' days (Months 4–6)

BUDGET SUMMARY:
  Materials & tools:     $8,500
  Labor & contractors:   $6,000
  Programming:           $2,000
  Contingency (10%):     $1,650
  TOTAL:                $18,150

FUNDING REQUEST: $18,150 from City Community Fund

EXPECTED IMPACT: 200+ residents served annually
